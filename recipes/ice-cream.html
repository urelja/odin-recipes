5 Minute Ice Cream

Description

This fruit ice cream is a fast, frozen treat that doesn't lack quality. It is an easy 5-minute recipe to wow last-minute guests with.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅔ cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Combine frozen strawberries and sugar in a food processor or blender. Process until strawberries are roughly chopped.
  2. With the processor on, slowly pour in heavy cream until incorporated. Store in the freezer for up to 1 week.
